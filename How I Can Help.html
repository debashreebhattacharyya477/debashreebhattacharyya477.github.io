How I Can Help

Ways I Support People & Organisations

I work with organisations, founders and HR teams to strengthen their people practices. Below are some of the areas where I can contribute. These are flexible – depending on the context, we can shape them as advisory support, project work or hands-on execution.

Recruitment & Hiring Support ➜
I help organisations structure and run hiring processes that are clear, fair and efficient – from defining the role to closing the offer. I particularly enjoy supporting opportunities for women returning to work and people whose roles were impacted by restructuring.
Role definition Screening & interviews Offer coordination
HR Policies, SOPs & Handbooks ➜
Creating simple, practical policies and SOPs that employees can actually understand and use. This includes drafting or refining HR handbooks, process documents and communication templates.
Policy drafting Process mapping Employee handbooks
HRMS / HCM Implementation ➜
Supporting selection, setup and rollout of HR systems – from basic HRMS to more advanced HCM tools. Focus is on clean data, intuitive workflows and reducing manual work for HR and managers.
Requirement gathering Configuration support Process automation
Performance & Feedback Practices ➜
Designing or refining performance review cycles, feedback rhythms and goal-setting practices so that employees know what is expected and managers have structure to support them.
Goal setting Review cycles Manager guidance
Inclusion, Diversity & Safe Workplaces ➜
Working on practices that encourage inclusive workplaces – from awareness sessions to basic frameworks around dignity at work, respectful communication and fair opportunities.
Inclusion focus Workplace behaviour Simple frameworks
HR Operations & Employee Experience ➜
Strengthening day-to-day HR operations – onboarding, documentation, query handling and communication – so that employees have a smooth and predictable experience throughout their journey.
Onboarding Employee queries Process clean-up
